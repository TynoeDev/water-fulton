About Wedrill

Reliable Water Drilling & Borehole Solutions in Gauteng

Wedrill is a trusted provider of comprehensive water solutions, specializing in borehole drilling, well rehabilitation, pump installation, and water treatment. We serve residential, commercial, agricultural, and industrial clients throughout Gauteng, South Africa.

Commitment to Quality and Customer Satisfaction

We are dedicated to providing high-quality workmanship and exceptional customer service. Our team uses premium materials and follows best practices to ensure long-lasting and reliable water solutions.
